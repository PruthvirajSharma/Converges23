A National Level Technical SYMPOSIUM

Name of Event:- Robo Race

Rules

Introduction
The ROBO RACE is the Technical event in which the participant have to run their ROBO on the track. The purpose of this event is to get the practical knowledge about robotics.

First Round
Run the robo through obstacles and complete the track.

Description
In this round the team have to run their robo through the obstacles on the track. The teams who complete the first round in minimum time period without the fouls will be eligible for next round.

Second Round
Return the robo from same track.

Description
In this round the team have to Run the robo return through obstacles and complete the same track.


Rules and Fees

Rules:
• Robot must have maximum size of 30x30x25cm.
• Robot should be prepared by the team member only, readymade chassis will not be allowed
• Maximum 12 DC voltage is allowed.
• Robot can be wired or wireless.
• Minimum length of wire should be about 6m.
• The wire must be kept slack when the robot is on the track.
• While on the track, no one is allowed to touch the robot except the volunteer.
• Any damage to the arena by the robot or by the team member will be disqualify the team.
• If hand touch is needed a member from team should call out for a “Hand Touch”.
• For every hand touch, team will be penalized for 10 sec.
• Team can skip the obstacle section with an additional penalty of 30 sec.
• If robot touches the wooden block team will be penalized by 10 sec per block.
• TOTAL TIME= (TIME REQUIRED TO COMPLETE THE TRACK )+(PENALTY)
• The team completing the task with minimum time will have advantage.
• Any change in the rules if any, it would be announced as and when required.
• Rules and decision made by judges or event head will be final and binding to all.
• Robot should have the maximum weight of 2 kg, excess of weight will leads to disqualification.
• For Round 1st :- Skip =3 Hand touch =3 • For Round 2nd:- Skip =3 Hand touch= 3
